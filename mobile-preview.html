A simple and efficient workflow to bring your vision to life. From the first call to final delivery, every step is designed for clarity and efficiency.

Foundations

Every builder starts here. You'll learn to ask AI for what you actually want, read errors without panic, find your way around your editor, and start spotting sharp code from sloppy — one small win at a time.

2

Context

Now you're feeding AI the right fuel. Set up context that sticks, write specs that get followed, structure projects so the model (and future you) can navigate them.

3

Develop

The tier where you ship. End-to-end flows, debugging strategies, handling edge cases, refactoring with intent — turning prompts into products.

4

Expert

The tier you earn. Think in user personas, stretch context windows, orchestrate multi-step agents, mentor others through their first ship.
